Welcome to my profile!

Summary

Colin studied at the University of Wisconsin - Madison, where he proved himself not only an avid Badger sports fan, but also a brilliant cheesemaker with a specialty in punching the holes into Swiss cheese. In 2005, Colin's groundbreaking cheese hole punching technique garnered him international acclaim and he was awarded the Cheez-It Cheese Maker of the Year award. Following this lofty recognition, Colin went on to co-found CheeseMe, Inc. - the world's #1 cheese producer - with his friends, Michael Chum Monty and Brady Nicholas Barton. He now lives in Minnesota and enjoys spending time at his cabin near Madison, Wisconsin with his family and friends.

Colin has leveraged his success with CheeseMe, Inc. to enter the affordable housing industry as an affordable housing developer and advocate. Through his development company, Ethos Housing Group, Colin has developed more than 20,000 affordable housing units across the country. He has also testfied in front of the US Congress on affordable housing issues and has been cited in more than 200 publications regarding the industry.
